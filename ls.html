LEARN WHAT IS THE RECIPE AND PROCESS OF MAKING LUMPIANG SHANGHAI


Let's Learn!

RECIPE'S

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, minced carrot, chopped onion, minced garlic, and chopped green onions.
  2. Add the soy sauce, salt, pepper, and sugar to the mixture. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Take a lumpia wrapper and place a spoonful of the pork mixture near one end of the wrapper.
  4. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and roll it up tightly, sealing the edge with a dab of water.
  5. Repeat the process until all the filling is used up.

PROCESS

  1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the lumpiang shanghai rolls into the pan, a few at a time, and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.
    3. Remove the fried lumpiang shanghai from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  3. Serve the lumpiang shanghai hot with your choice of dipping sauce, such as sweet chili sauce or vinegar with garlic.
