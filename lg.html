LEARN WHAT IS THE RECIPE AND PROCESS OF MAKING LUMPIANG GULAY


Let's Learn!

RECIPE'S

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, shrimp, carrots, cabbage, green onions, garlic, soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  • Take a lumpia wrapper and place a spoonful of the filling mixture onto the center of the wrapper. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and roll it tightly, sealing the edges with a dab of water.
  • Repeat the process until all the filling is used up.

PROCESS

  1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the lumpiang gulay rolls into the pan, a few at a time, and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.
    3. Remove the fried lumpiang gulay from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  3. Serve the lumpiang gulay hot with your choice of dipping sauce, such as sweet chili sauce or vinegar with garlic.
